# CLARA P18 Runtime Trial Admin Checklist

Before trial:
- Confirm P17 Real AI Analysis Activation is complete for controlled internal use.
- Confirm P18 Controlled Internal Runtime Trial + Operational Readiness is current.
- Confirm P18-PR-01 is complete.
- Confirm P18-PR-02 is current.
- Confirm AI provider secrets remain server-only.
- Confirm extension must not call AI providers directly.
- Confirm P18 is not public SaaS launch.
- Confirm P18 is not production deployment.
- Confirm P18 does not activate billing/payment.
- Confirm P18 does not activate official WA/IG/TikTok APIs.
- Confirm P18 does not enable outbound auto-send.

After trial:
- Review evidence privacy.
- Review stop criteria.
- Review manual rollback guidance.
- Review known limitations before broader rollout.
