# 01 — Product Context

> *"CLARA starts by helping teams handle customer conversations better, not by trying to automate the whole business on day one."*

---

# Background

CLARA is designed as an AI-native business operating system.

Long-term, CLARA may include:

```text
CRM
omnichannel communication
AI assistant
knowledge system
workflow automation
analytics
customer operations
team roles
integrations
```

But the first buildable slice must be smaller.

---

# Why Start with Conversation Inbox

Customer conversations are a strong first slice because they connect many CLARA concepts:

```text
customer identity
conversation history
team workflow
AI drafting
human review
activity logging
future CRM
future support operations
future automation
```

---

# Product Bet

The MVP bet is:

```text
If CLARA can help a user respond to customer conversations faster and safer with customer context and AI draft assistance, then CLARA has a credible foundation for future CRM and automation workflows.
```

---

# Current Pain Points

Target users may experience:

```text
too many browser tabs
manual copy-paste between tools
inconsistent replies
forgotten follow-up
customer context scattered in notes
slow response time
unclear ownership of conversations
unsafe AI use outside controlled workspace
```

---

# Product Opportunity

CLARA can create value by combining:

```text
customer conversation
customer profile
AI draft
human review
activity history
team access control
```

---

# MVP Design Philosophy

The MVP should be:

```text
small
safe
observable
reviewable
human-controlled
easy to demo
easy to extend
```

# Production Mindset

Even though this is MVP, treat it as production-intent.

That means:

```text
no hard-coded secrets
no client-only authorization
no unsafe AI auto-send
no raw sensitive logs
no unclear ownership
no unbounded scope creep
```
