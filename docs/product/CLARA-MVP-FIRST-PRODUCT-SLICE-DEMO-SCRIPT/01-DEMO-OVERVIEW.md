# 01 — Demo Overview

> *"The demo should prove one clean product loop instead of showing too many unfinished features."*

---

# Demo Name

```text
CLARA MVP — Human-Reviewed AI Conversation Reply
```

---

# What We Are Demonstrating

```text
Unified customer conversation workspace
Customer profile context
AI-assisted reply draft
Human review before send
Simulated reply send
Activity timeline
Viewer restrictions
Safe fallback behavior
```

---

# What We Are Not Demonstrating Yet

```text
full omnichannel production integration
autonomous AI agent
bulk messaging
campaign automation
advanced CRM pipeline
billing
mobile app
enterprise admin
real external provider delivery
```

---

# Positioning

```text
This is the first product slice of CLARA. It is intentionally focused. We are validating the safest and most valuable customer communication loop before expanding into integrations, automation, and advanced CRM.
```

---

# Product Promise

```text
CLARA helps teams respond faster without giving AI unchecked authority.
```

---

# Technical Promise

```text
The system is designed with server-side authorization, workspace scoping, AI context minimization, safe error handling, and activity traceability from day one.
```

---

# Rule

```text
Keep the demo focused on the MVP slice. Do not overpromise future automation.
```
