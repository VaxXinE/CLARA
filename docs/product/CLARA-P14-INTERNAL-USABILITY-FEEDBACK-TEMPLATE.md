## Privacy Rule

Feedback should minimize customer-sensitive data. Do not include secrets,
tokens, cookies, auth headers, raw provider payloads, raw webhook payloads, raw
HTML, raw DOM, raw prompts, payment data, or full customer transcripts.

Usability feedback is for internal beta only and does not trigger external
support integration, outbound messaging, provider calls, AI execution, or
billing behavior.
