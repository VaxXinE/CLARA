## Participant Rules

- Participants must use assigned internal accounts and roles only.
- Viewers stay read-only and must not be used to validate mutation flows.
- Operators validate CRM workflow execution, not admin/security ownership.
- Admins/owners validate access setup, security checklist, and stop/go gates.
- Participants must report issues with redacted evidence only.
