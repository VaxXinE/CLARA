# CLARA P12 Support / Feedback Readiness Boundary

P12-PR-01 defines the support boundary only. P12-PR-04 will define the detailed
Beta feedback, support, and known issues workflow.

## Beta Support Boundary

- Beta users must be known and limited.
- Feedback must be manually triaged.
- Known issues must be tracked before expanding access.
- Incidents must use manual operator review.
- Support messages must not include secrets, tokens, raw provider payloads, raw
  customer messages, raw prompts, raw payment data, or raw telemetry.

## Not Implemented Here

- Support ticket product UI.
- Automated incident execution.
- Evidence export.
- Billing support automation.
- Provider support automation.
- AI-generated customer-facing support replies.
