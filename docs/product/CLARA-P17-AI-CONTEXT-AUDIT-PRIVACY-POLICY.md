# CLARA P17 AI Context Audit Privacy Policy

P17-PR-02 is current.

## Audit Privacy

Audit, logs, and docs may reference safe policy names, safe reason codes, scoped
resource identifiers, bounded counts, and timestamps. They must not include raw
prompts, raw customer messages, raw AI provider payloads and responses, raw
provider payloads, raw webhook payloads, tokens, cookies, auth headers, API
keys, secrets, or payment data.

AI provider secrets are server-only. AI provider secrets must not be exposed to
dashboard or extension.

## Product Boundaries

Billing/payment remains deferred. CLARA is not public SaaS launch. CLARA is not
production deployment claim unless separately executed. Official WA/IG/TikTok
APIs remain not activated.
