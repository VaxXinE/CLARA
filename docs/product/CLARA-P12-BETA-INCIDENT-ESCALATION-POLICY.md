# CLARA P12 Beta Incident Escalation Policy

P12-PR-01 is complete. P12-PR-02 is complete. P12-PR-03 is complete. P12-PR-04 is current.

CLARA is not GA yet.

The beta feedback workflow is controlled and privacy-safe.

Feedback/support must not collect raw sensitive data.

No external support tool integration happens in this PR.

No auto-send or external ticket creation happens in this PR.

No provider/payment/AI/outbound activation happens in this PR.

## Escalation

- S0: escalate to security, engineering, product, and operations immediately.
- S1: escalate to engineering and product owner.
- S2: assign owner and workaround review.
- S3/S4: batch into weekly beta review.

Escalation is documented manually in the release evidence.

This PR does not send alerts, emails, Slack messages, Discord messages, or webhooks.
