## After P12

After this PR, P12 may be marked complete only as release readiness complete.
P12 completion means release readiness complete. P12 completion does not mean
production deployed. P12 completion does not mean public GA launch happened.

Before any real production deployment:

1. Record final go/no-go.
2. Obtain separate explicit approval and execution for production deployment.
3. Confirm known limitations are accepted or resolved.
4. Confirm no no-go blocker remains.
5. Confirm provider/payment/AI/outbound activation remains restricted unless
   future approved work enables it.
6. Confirm readiness-only/review-only/simulated/demo-safe boundaries remain
   intact.

Production launch is a future operational and business decision, not an
automatic result of P12 completion.
