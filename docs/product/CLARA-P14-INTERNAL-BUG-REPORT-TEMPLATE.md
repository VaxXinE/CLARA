## Do Not Include

Feedback must not include secrets/tokens/cookies/auth headers/raw provider
payload/raw webhook payload/raw HTML/raw DOM/raw prompts/payment data. Feedback
should minimize customer-sensitive data.

Use redacted examples such as `customer@example.test` and safe internal IDs.
Never paste access tokens, refresh tokens, cookies, provider responses, raw
webhook bodies, payment details, or full customer conversations.
