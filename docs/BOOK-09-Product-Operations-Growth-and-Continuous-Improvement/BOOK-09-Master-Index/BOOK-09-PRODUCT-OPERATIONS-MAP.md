# Product Operations Model

```mermaid
flowchart TD
    Customer[Customer Evidence] --> Feedback[Feedback Operating Model]
    Feedback --> Metrics[Product Metrics]
    Metrics --> Risk[Risk and Trust Review]
    Risk --> Decision[Product Decision]
    Decision --> Owner[Owner / RACI]
    Owner --> Cadence[Review Cadence]
    Cadence --> Evidence[Documentation Evidence]
    Evidence --> Improve[Continuous Improvement]
    Improve --> Customer
```
